About Us

About Me
My name is Max Billings, and I started Yellow Jacket Lawn Care with a passion for hard work, attention to detail, and helping people take pride in their outdoor spaces. What began with just a mower and a few neighborhood lawns has quickly grown into a professional business built on reliability and quality. I take pride in treating every yard as if it were my own, making sure my clients know they can count on me week after week.

About the Business
Yellow Jacket Lawn Care is a locally owned and operated lawn care service based in Lexington, KY. We specialize in mowing, trimming, edging, and cleanups, while also offering seasonal services such as aeration, overseeding, fertilization, and leaf removal. With a focus on residential properties and small landscapes, our goal is to provide homeowners with lush, healthy lawns that look great year-round. We use dependable equipment and proven techniques to get the job done right, no shortcuts.

Our Promise
At Yellow Jacket Lawn Care, customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do. Whether it’s maintaining your yard on a weekly schedule or preparing your property for the changing seasons, we strive to deliver dependable service and visible results. By choosing Yellow Jacket Lawn Care, you’re not just hiring someone to cut grass—you’re partnering with a business that values honesty, consistency, and the pride of a well-kept lawn.
